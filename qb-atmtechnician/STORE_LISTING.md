## Description

Turn ATM maintenance into a full legal career on your server! Players
apply at the Job HQ, suit up, and take random repair shifts across the
map — with real consequences, real tools, and real risk of getting
flagged by police.

**🛠 What's Included**
- **Shift System** — 5 random ATMs assigned per shift out of 20 mapped
  locations, revealed one at a time so players have to actually drive
  the city
- **Skillcheck Minigame** — fail it and risk breaking a tool or getting
  a police alert
- **Job Vehicle** — dedicated garage, spawn/store logic, and a van
  repair kit item
- **Tool Shop** — buy toolbox, tablet, screwdriver, wire cutter,
  diagnostic scanner, and masterkey from the job NPC
- **Job Tablet (NUI)** — stats, recent job history, and in-tablet
  shopping
- **Auto Uniform System** — clean on-apply / on-quit clothing swap that
  restores the player's original outfit exactly
- **ID Card Item** — auto given/removed with the job
- **Police Integration** — configurable alert chance on suspicious
  activity or masterkey use
- **Full SQL Logging** — per-ATM cooldowns and complete job history
- **English + Tamil locales** included, fully translatable
- **Escrow protected** — safe from leaks/resale

**⚙️ Requirements**
- QBCore Framework
- qb-target
- oxmysql

**📦 Easy Setup**
Import the SQL file, add the job to `qb-core/shared/jobs.lua`, drop the
required items into `qb-core/shared/items.lua`, adjust `config.lua` to
your map, and you're live in under 10 minutes.
